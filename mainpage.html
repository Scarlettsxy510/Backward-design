After completing this module, students should be able to:

  • Understanding varieties of Chinese language.
    • Recall the sound system of Mandarin Chinese(pinyin and tones).
    • Identify simplified and traditional forms of Chinese characters.
  • Introducing oneself appropriately.
    • Express names and nationalities.
    • Describe family relationships and members.
  • Communicating about preferences, likes and dislikes.
    • Discuss favorite colors.
    • Tell preferred activities.
